# Qurmet Hall Website Concept and Strategy

This document outlines the complete concept for the Qurmet Hall website, including the sitemap, wireframe description, design guidelines, and content strategy, based on the visual identity of the restaurant as a grand banquet hall in Aktobe.

## 1. Sitemap (Website Structure)

The website will be a multi-page site, focusing on the primary business of hosting large events and banquets.

| Page Title | URL Path | Primary Goal | SEO Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Home** | `/index.html` | Immediately convey the grandeur and primary service (banquet hall). | "Банкетный зал Актобе", "Ресторан для мероприятий Актобе" |
| **О нас (About Us)** | `/about.html` | Detail the restaurant's concept, capacity, and history. | "Лучший банкетный зал", "Проведение торжеств Актобе" |
| **Залы (Halls)** | `/halls.html` | Showcase the different banquet halls with photos and capacity details. | "Большой банкетный зал на 500 человек", "Залы для свадьбы Актобе" |
| **Меню (Menu)** | `/menu.html` | Present sample banquet menus and catering options. | "Банкетное меню Актобе", "Кухня для торжеств" |
| **Галерея (Gallery)** | `/gallery.html` | Visual showcase of past events, interior, and exterior. | "Фотографии банкетного зала Курмет Холл" |
| **Контакты (Contact)** | `/contact.html` | Provide location, contact details, and a booking form. | "Забронировать банкетный зал Актобе", "Контакты Курмет Холл" |

## 2. Design and Style Guidelines

The design will be **Elegant and Grand**, reflecting the restaurant's identity as a high-end banquet complex.

### Color Palette
The palette is inspired by the luxurious interior and exterior architecture.

| Color | Hex Code | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary** (White/Cream) | `#F5F5F5` | Backgrounds, main content areas. |
| **Accent 1** (Gold/Yellow) | `#C8A25C` | Headings, primary call-to-action buttons, decorative elements. |
| **Accent 2** (Deep Burgundy) | `#800020` | Hover states, secondary call-to-action buttons, key text highlights. |
| **Text** (Dark Gray) | `#333333` | Main body text for high readability. |

### Typography
A combination of a classic serif for a luxurious feel and a clean sans-serif for modern readability.

| Font | Type | Usage | Recommendation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Serif** | Display/Headings | H1, H2, main titles. | Playfair Display (Google Font) |
| **Sans-serif** | Body/UI | Paragraphs, navigation, buttons. | Montserrat or Roboto (Google Font) |

## 3. Wireframe Description (Home Page)

The Home Page (`index.html`) will follow a classic, full-screen, and modular layout to emphasize visual impact and key information.

1.  **Header/Navigation**:
    *   **Logo**: "Qurmet Hall" in the top left, using the gold accent color.
    *   **Navigation**: Links to **О нас**, **Залы**, **Меню**, **Галерея**, **Контакты**.
    *   **CTA Button**: "Забронировать" (Book) in the top right, using the deep burgundy color.
2.  **Hero Section (Full-Screen)**:
    *   **Background**: High-quality, full-screen image of the grand interior (Image 2 or 5 from assets).
    *   **Overlay Text**: Large, elegant H1: "Qurmet Hall: Искусство Вашего Торжества" (Qurmet Hall: The Art of Your Celebration).
    *   **Subtext**: "Банкетный зал вместимостью до 500 гостей в Актобе."
    *   **Primary CTA**: Large "Забронировать Дату" (Book a Date) button.
3.  **About/Concept Section**:
    *   **Layout**: Two-column section. Text on the left, a smaller, elegant interior photo on the right.
    *   **Content**: Short, compelling text about the concept (see Section 4).
    *   **Secondary CTA**: "Узнать больше о нас" (Learn more about us).
4.  **Halls/Capacity Preview**:
    *   **Title**: "Наши Залы" (Our Halls).
    *   **Content**: Three-column grid showcasing the main hall and any smaller halls (if applicable), with capacity and a small photo for each.
5.  **Gallery/Instagram Feed**:
    *   **Title**: "Моменты Роскоши" (Moments of Luxury).
    *   **Content**: A carousel/grid of 4-6 photos (simulating the Instagram feed).
6.  **Contact/Booking Teaser**:
    *   **Title**: "Ваш Идеальный Праздник Начинается Здесь."
    *   **Content**: A brief call to action with a mini-booking form (Name, Phone, Event Type).
7.  **Footer**:
    *   Logo, Address, Phone, Email, Social Media links (Instagram, Facebook), and a quick navigation menu.
